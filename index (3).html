your 6 rules
01
1 workout every day 45–75 min
Gym 5x/week (strength focus) + 2 active recovery days (walk, yoga, swim). At least 3 sessions must be lifting. No full rest days — active rest only.
02
3 liters of water daily before 9pm
Not the full gallon of 75 Hard, but 3L is transformative for skin, energy, and appetite control. Track it with a marked bottle or three 1L fills.
03
Follow your eating plan 6 days clean
High protein (0.8g per lb bodyweight), whole foods, caloric deficit on rest days. 1 flexible meal on Sunday — not a cheat day. Zero alcohol for 75 days.
04
Read 10 pages daily non-fiction
Straight from 75 Hard and it works. No phone reading. Kindle or physical book. Pick something genuinely exciting, not a chore. 10 pages = ~15 mins with morning coffee.
05
No phone before 10am or after 9pm dopamine rule
Morning phone use hijacks your cortisol response and kills focus for hours. Evening use destroys sleep quality and skin. Use those windows for your stack instead.
06
5-min evening journal + 1 thing learned learning rule
Every night: 3 wins from today, 1 thing you genuinely learned, tomorrow's top 3 priorities. Ties your dopamine detox and learning goals into one daily ritual.
The 75 Medium miss rule
If you miss a rule, you don't restart. Log it in the Today tab, write one honest sentence about why, and go harder tomorrow. No shame spiral — just data and accountability.
